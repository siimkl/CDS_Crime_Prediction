Methodology

How the two-year simulation works

  • Start with the 28 Jan 2026 to 28 Apr 2026 suburb-level incident baseline.
  • Simulate eight quarters of patrol allocation, with more persistent attention in targeted minority and socially vulnerable areas.
  • Feed each quarter's patrol pattern back into the next quarter's recorded incident data to estimate longer-run winners, losers, and social side effects.
