Important notice

Prototype system notice

Academic prototype only

This application is a prototype decision-support tool developed for academic project work. It is not an operational police information system.

Critical framing

It should also be understood as a critical demonstration of why systems of this kind require careful scrutiny, and how they can be misleading or inappropriate when presented as ready-made support for real public-safety decision-making.

Operational limitation

Forecasts, risk scores, and recommended strategies shown here are illustrative and should not be relied upon for real-world policing, emergency response, or public-safety decisions.
